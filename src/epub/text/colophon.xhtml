Colophon

Lyrical Ballads
was published in 1798 by
William Wordsworth and Samuel Taylor Coleridge.
This ebook edition is based on a revised version published in 1805.

This ebook was produced for
Standard Ebooks
by
Alex Cabal,
and is based on transcriptions produced in 2001 by
Bruce Graver and Ronald Tetreault
for
Romantic Circles
and on digital scans from the
Internet Archive.

The cover page is adapted from
Der Wanderer über dem Nebelmeer,
a painting completed in 1818 by
Caspar David Friedrich.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.

The first edition of this ebook was released on
May 25, 2014, 12:00 a.m.
